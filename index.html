ABOUT US

Albay Dialects Hub: Fostering Inclusive Community Development

Language plays a crucial role in defining culture and identity, and the diverse range of dialects in the Albay region adds unique flavors to our local culture. This project aims to develop an inclusive online platform for learning the various dialects spoken across Albay, fostering unity among communities and preserving the linguistic tapestry of the region.
